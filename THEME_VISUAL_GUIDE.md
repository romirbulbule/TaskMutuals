## ğŸ¨ Color Accessibility

All color combinations meet **WCAG AA standards** for contrast:

âœ… **Light Mode:**
- Forest Green (#2C4F41) on Cream (#F5EDE4) = **5.8:1** ratio
- Dark Green text (#233C32) on Cream = **8.2:1** ratio

âœ… **Dark Mode:**
- Cream (#F5EDE4) on Forest Green (#2C4F41) = **5.8:1** ratio
- Cream text on Forest Green = **5.8:1** ratio

All text is **readable and accessible** for users with visual impairments.
